About Renew

Transforming Neighborhoods, One Street at a Time

The Renew project began in Sadashivnagar, Bangalore, with a simple vision: to transform a high-profile residential area into a model of urban excellence. What started as addressing house numbering inconsistencies has evolved into a comprehensive neighborhood enhancement initiative.

Our approach is holistic - we believe that true transformation begins at the individual home level but extends to the entire community. From standardized door numbers and security systems to rainwater harvesting and beautiful streetscapes, we've redefined what urban living can be.

At Renew, we challenge the notion that streets are "no man's land" - instead, we see them as "every man's land," shared spaces that deserve care, attention, and beauty. Our team has proven that collaborative effort can create extraordinary neighborhoods.

Our Services

Home Identification

We install standardized door numbers and building plates, ensuring every home is easily identifiable for deliveries, services, and emergency response.

Security Enhancement

From doorbell cameras to integrated CCTV systems with centralized monitoring, we make neighborhoods safer through technology.

Water Conservation

Our rainwater harvesting solutions help buildings conserve water, reduce bills, and contribute to environmental sustainability.

Street Lighting

We install aesthetic street lighting to improve safety, enhance visual appeal, and create welcoming community spaces.

Landscaping

From maintaining existing green spaces to creating new parks, we add nature back into urban environments.

Street Maintenance

We repair potholes, maintain clean pavements, and ensure streets are well-maintained and beautiful.
